# AI Voice Calling System - Business Opportunities

## What We Built

Automated AI phone calls that:

- Call businesses/contacts from a list
- Ask specific questions (availability, pricing, services)
- Record conversations and extract structured data
- Save results to Excel with transcripts and recordings

---

## High-Value Target Markets

### 1. Healthcare Data Companies

- Verify doctor availability, insurance acceptance, services
- Keep medical directories updated
- Update specialist availability for referrals
- **Potential Clients:** Zocdoc, Healthgrades, Discovery Health, Momentum, medical aid schemes

### 2. Lead Generation Agencies

- Qualify leads before passing to sales teams
- "Does your company need X service?" type calls
- Pre-book appointments for sales reps
- Verify decision-maker contact info
- **Potential Clients:** B2B sales agencies, marketing agencies

### 3. Real Estate

- Call landlords/agents for availability
- Gather rental prices and terms
- Verify listing accuracy
- **Potential Clients:** Property24, Private Property, rental agencies, property management companies

### 4. Recruitment Agencies

- Screen candidates with basic questions
- Schedule interviews
- Verify availability and salary expectations
- Initial candidate outreach
- **Potential Clients:** Pnet clients, recruitment agencies, HR departments

### 5. Market Research Firms

- Price surveys across competitors
- Mystery shopping calls
- Verify business hours/services
- Competitive intelligence gathering
- **Potential Clients:** Nielsen, research agencies, competitive intelligence firms

### 6. Insurance Companies

- Verify provider networks
- Claims follow-up calls
- Policy renewal reminders
- Verify customer details
- **Potential Clients:** Discovery, Santam, OUTsurance, Hollard

### 7. Directory & Data Services

- Verify business information accuracy
- Update contact databases
- Confirm operating hours and services
- **Potential Clients:** Yellow Pages, Google Local Services partners, Yelp equivalents

### 8. Appointment-Based Businesses

- Dental practices finding specialists
- Veterinary clinics
- Auto service centers
- Salon/spa availability checks

## Pricing Models

### Per-Call Pricing

- R5-R10 per attempted call
- R15-R25 per completed call with data extracted
- Premium for calls over 2 minutes

### Monthly Subscription

- Starter: R2,000/month - 100 calls
- Growth: R5,000/month - 300 calls
- Enterprise: R15,000/month - 1000 calls

### Enterprise/Custom

- Dedicated assistant voice
- Custom integrations (CRM, databases)
- White-label solution

---

## Competitive Advantages

1. **Local SA number** - Higher answer rates than international numbers
2. **Natural conversation** - AI handles follow-up questions
3. **Structured data extraction** - Not just transcripts, actual usable data
4. **Recordings included** - Full audit trail
5. **Handles IVR systems** - Navigates "press 1 for..." menus
