## Risk Factors & Mitigation

### Risk 1: High Churn
**Mitigation:**
- Focus on high-volume users (daily need = sticky)
- Excellent onboarding (get value in first week)
- Regular check-ins (prevent churn)
- Customer success team
- **Target:** < 2% monthly churn

### Risk 2: Low Conversion
**Mitigation:**
- Free pilot (50 calls) - removes risk
- Case studies from early customers
- ROI calculator on landing page
- Dedicated sales team
- **Target:** 1.5% lead → customer conversion

### Risk 3: Competition
**Mitigation:**
- SA-focused (local numbers, ZAR pricing)
- Full platform (not just API)
- Better UX than DIY solutions
- **Differentiation:** Local, easy, proven ROI, dedicated support

### Risk 4: VAPI Cost Overruns
**Mitigation:**
- Monitor call costs closely
- Optimize call duration
- Pass some costs to customers if needed
- Volume discounts from VAPI
- **Target:** Keep VAPI costs < 12% of revenue

### Risk 5: Scaling Challenges
**Mitigation:**
- Hire dedicated support team early
- Build scalable infrastructure
- Automate onboarding
- Create self-service resources
- **Target:** Support 100 customers with < 5 support staff
