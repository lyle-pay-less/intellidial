## Part 2: People search (get contacts at those companies)

Go to **Apollo → Search → People**.

- **Company:** Your exported company list (upload or paste domains).  
- **Location:** Cape Town (same as company search).  

**Job titles (pick one set or mix):**

- Head of Recruitment, Recruitment Manager, Talent Acquisition, Managing Director, Founder, CEO  
- Founder, CEO, Sales Director, Head of Demand Gen, VP Sales  
- Operations Manager, Call Centre Manager, Contact Centre Manager  

**Seniority:** Manager, Director, VP, C-Level, Owner, Founder  

Export: Name, job title, company, email, LinkedIn.
