## Cost Comparison: Manual vs Intellidial

### Manual Calling Costs:
- **Caller salary:** R300/hour (R5/minute)
- **Average call:** 3-5 minutes = **R15-25 per call**
- **Plus:** Training, management, turnover, infrastructure

### Intellidial Cost:
- **R9.99/call** (Growth plan)
- **R8.99/call** (Pro plan)

### Savings per Call:
- **R5-15 saved per call** vs manual
- For 1,000 calls/month: **R5,000-15,000 saved**

**Why it works:**

1. **Clear ROI:**
   - Manual: R15-25/call

## Recommended Pricing Adjustments

### Option 1: Add Higher Tiers (Recommended)

| Plan | Price/Month | Calls/Month | Cost per Call | Best For |
|------|-------------|-------------|---------------|----------|
| Starter | R999 | 100 | R9.99 | Testing, small projects |
| Growth | R2,999 | 300 | R9.99 | Small agencies |
| **Pro** | **R8,999** | **1,000** | **R8.99** | Medium agencies |
| **Business** | **R19,999** | **3,000** | **R6.67** | **High-volume (NEW)** |
| **Enterprise** | **R39,999** | **10,000** | **R4.00** | **Very high-volume (NEW)** |

**Why this works:**
- Business tier (R19,999 for 3,000 calls) fits lead gen/telemarketing companies
- Enterprise tier (R39,999 for 10,000 calls) fits largest users
- Volume discounts (R6.67/call, R4/call) reward high usage

### Option 2: Usage-Based Overage

Keep current plans, add:
- **Overage:** R10/call after plan limit
- Example: Growth plan (300 calls) + 700 overage = R2,999 + R7,000 = R9,999 total

**Pros:** Flexible, pay-as-you-scale
**Cons:** Harder to predict costs

### Option 3: Custom Enterprise Pricing

- **Pro plan:** R8,999 for 1,000 calls
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing for 2,000+ calls
- **Negotiate:** R6-8/call for high volume

**Pros:** Flexible, can negotiate per client
**Cons:** More sales work, less transparent
