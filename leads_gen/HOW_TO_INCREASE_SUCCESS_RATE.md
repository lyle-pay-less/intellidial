**Action Items:**
- [ ] **Day 1-2:** Finish remaining 15% of product

  - Fix build errors (`npm run build` working)
  - E2E validation (test real call → results)
  
- [ ] **Day 3-4:** Build self-serve trial flow
  - Landing page "Start Free Trial" button

- [ ] **Day 5-7:** Deploy to production

  - SSL/HTTPS verified

**Priority:** CRITICAL — Do this first

### 3. Focus on Customer Success (+5% Success Rate)

**Why:** High churn kills SaaS businesses. Customer success = retention = growth.

**Action Items:**
- [ ] **Onboarding:**
  - Welcome email immediately
  - Quick start guide (5 steps)
  - Video walkthrough (5 min)
  - First project setup help
  
- [ ] **Show ROI Immediately:**
  - "You've saved 5 hours" message
  - "You've made 50 calls" dashboard
  - Success metrics visible
  
- [ ] **Regular Check-ins:**
  - Day 2: "How's it going?"
  - Day 7: "Ready to upgrade?"
  - Week 2: "Any questions?"
  - Month 1: "How can we help?"

- [ ] **Reduce Churn:**
  - Identify at-risk customers (low usage)
  - Reach out before they cancel
  - Offer help, not just upsell
  - Make cancellation hard (but fair)

**Impact:** +5% success probability  
**Timeline:** Start Week 1, ongoing  
**Priority:** HIGH — Retention = growth

### 4. Execute Consistently (+5% Success Rate)

**Why:** Most founders give up after first setback. Consistency wins.

**Action Items:**
- [ ] **Don't Give Up:**
  - First customer rejection? Keep going
  - Product bug? Fix it, move on
  - Agent quits? Hire another
  - Setback = learning, not failure
  
- [ ] **Iterate Based on Feedback:**
  - Ask customers: "What's missing?"
  - Fix top 3 issues immediately
  - Don't build features no one wants
  - Focus on what converts
  
- [ ] **Stay Focused:**
  - Don't chase shiny objects
  - Focus on one thing at a time
  - Finish before starting new
  - Say no to distractions

- [ ] **Track Progress:**
  - Weekly metrics review
  - What's working? Do more
  - What's not? Fix or stop
  - Celebrate small wins

**Impact:** +5% success probability  
**Timeline:** Ongoing  
**Priority:** HIGH — This is the difference maker
