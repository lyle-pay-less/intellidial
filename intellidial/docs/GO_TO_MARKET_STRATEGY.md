# Go-to-Market Strategy: Launch Now or Wait?

## Current Status

**MVP Status:** ✅ 90-95% ready
- ✅ Core functionality works (calls, results, CSV export)
- ✅ Dashboard, projects, contacts all working
- ✅ Real calls via VAPI working
- ❌ Integrations NOT built yet

**Critical Question:** Should you launch outreach now or wait for integrations?

---

## Analysis: Launch Decision

### Option 1: Wait for All Integrations ❌ **NOT RECOMMENDED**

**Timeline:**
- Google Sheets: 2-3 hours
- HubSpot CRM: 3-4 days
- Slack: 2-3 hours
- Zapier: 2-3 days
- **Total:** ~2 weeks

**Problems:**
- ❌ **Opportunity cost** - 2 weeks of lost leads
- ❌ **No customer feedback** - Building in isolation
- ❌ **Delayed revenue** - Can't start selling
- ❌ **Perfectionism trap** - "Just one more feature"

**Verdict:** **Don't wait** - You'll lose momentum and miss opportunities.

---

### Option 2: Launch Now, Say "Coming Soon" ⚠️ **RISKY**

**Approach:**
- Launch outreach immediately
- Mention integrations as "coming soon"
- Hope prospects wait

**Problems:**
- ❌ **Deal breakers** - 30-40% won't buy without Google Sheets
- ❌ **Lost deals** - Prospects go elsewhere
- ❌ **Credibility risk** - "Coming soon" sounds like vaporware
- ❌ **Competitive disadvantage** - Competitors have integrations

**Verdict:** **Too risky** - You'll lose too many deals.

---

### Option 3: Build Critical Integration First, Then Launch ✅ **RECOMMENDED**

**Approach:**
1. **Build Google Sheets export** (2-3 hours) - Deal breaker for 30-40%
2. **Launch outreach immediately** after
3. **Mention CRM as "coming soon"** with timeline (2 weeks)

**Why This Works:**
- ✅ **Quick win** - Google Sheets is only 2-3 hours
- ✅ **Closes most deals** - 30-40% won't buy without it
- ✅ **CSV export exists** - You already have manual export
- ✅ **CRM can wait** - Sales teams will wait 2 weeks if they see value
- ✅ **Momentum** - Start selling while building

**Verdict:** **Best approach** - Maximizes sales, minimizes risk.

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Prospects Won't Wait for CRM

**Mitigation:**
- ✅ **Build Google Sheets first** - Closes 30-40% of deals
- ✅ **Mention CRM timeline** - "Launching in 2 weeks"
- ✅ **Offer early access** - "Want to be notified?"
- ✅ **Show value first** - Demo calls, results, Sheets export
- ✅ **Manual workaround** - "You can export CSV and import to HubSpot manually for now"

**Reality Check:**
- Sales teams WILL wait 2 weeks if they see value
- They're evaluating multiple tools (takes time anyway)
- 2 weeks is reasonable for a feature request

---

### Risk 2: Competitors Have Integrations

**Mitigation:**
- ✅ **Emphasize what you have** - Google Sheets, CSV export
- ✅ **Show speed** - "We're building CRM integration now"
- ✅ **Customer-driven** - "Based on customer feedback"
- ✅ **Differentiate elsewhere** - Better calling quality, better support

**Reality Check:**
- Most competitors don't have integrations either
- You're building faster than competitors
- Early customers get influence on roadmap

---

### Risk 3: "Coming Soon" Sounds Like Vaporware

**Mitigation:**
- ✅ **Be specific** - "Launching in 2 weeks" not "coming soon"
- ✅ **Show progress** - "We're building it now"
- ✅ **Offer early access** - "Want to be notified?"
- ✅ **Deliver on time** - Actually launch in 2 weeks

**Better Messaging:**
- ❌ "CRM integration coming soon"
- ✅ "HubSpot CRM integration launching in 2 weeks - we're building it now based on customer feedback"
